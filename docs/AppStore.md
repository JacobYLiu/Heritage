## Full Description

Heritage is a language learning app designed for heritage speakers — people who grew up hearing Chinese, Japanese, or Korean at home but never had the chance to fully learn it.

Unlike general language apps, Heritage focuses on the specific contexts you actually need: talking to grandparents, navigating family gatherings, understanding the words your parents use at home.

**Features:**
- AI-powered roleplay with a family character (grandmother, grandfather, parent, aunt/uncle) in realistic home scenarios
- Spaced-repetition flashcards focused on family vocabulary, food, health, and emotional expression
- Listening practice with comprehension questions
- Honorifics & family address terms guide — including Chinese maternal/paternal distinctions, Japanese honorific suffixes (さん、ちゃん、くん), and Korean speech levels (존댓말/반말)
- Passive skill model that improves silently without explicit scores
- Full Traditional and Simplified Chinese support
- Offline mode: download cards and audio for practice anywhere

**Languages supported:**
- Chinese (Mandarin) — Simplified & Traditional
- Japanese
- Korean

Heritage never corrects your grammar mid-conversation. It celebrates what you already know and helps you grow from there.
